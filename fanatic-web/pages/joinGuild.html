#JOIN GUILD

Fanatic consists of five legendary guilds. Each guild is named for the house founder. The guild virtues are those of its founder and members. By answering the questions below, we will determine your virtues, and place you into the most appropraite guild.

  • Seaknight Templars - Mariners, explorers, traders, and intellectuals. Founder Wharfrat Von Seaknight. Sigil Sea Kraken. House of Newfoundland receives a scientific bonus.
  • Wolfram Oxhaus - Masons and exceptional builders. Secretive in their trade and sedentary. Founder Sir Wolfram Oxhaus Sigil is the Ox. Members receive a construction bonus.
  • House of Bloodhoof - Agrarian society who values culture and magical creatures. Founder Edelnari Zoo Bloodhoof. Sigil is the centaur. Guild receives agriculture bonus.
  • Guilde de Saint Germaine - Warriors named after house founder Comte de Remy Saint Germain. Militeristic and purveyors of conquest. Sigil is the war bison. Guild receives military bonus.
  • Maccabee Canaan - Bankers and ruling elite. Conquest through economic means. Opportunistic and militant. Sigil goblin. Guild receives economic bonus.
