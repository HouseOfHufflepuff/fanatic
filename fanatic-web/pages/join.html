#JOIN FANATIC

Fanatic is a game where five legendary guilds battle a world for controlling power. Each player is placed into one of the five guilds based on thier style of rule. Like-minded players will combine to take over rival guilds through culture and warfare. The game continues real-time 24/7/365 until a single guild controls 60% of the worlds evolving population. Fanatic is the most complicated and rewarding MMORTS you'll ever play.
